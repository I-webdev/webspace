Construction

At HNL you get a team of professionals working to maximize the value of your project. Our project professionals are nimble and innovative, tailoring design and construction solutions to the best interest of our Customers. Our numerous repeat and new Customers leverage relationships we have earned over our four decades in business. You benefit from the relationships we have with designers and consultants.

We provide a comprehensive picture of project development costs rather than just a snapshot of site or building costs. Our Customers regularly rely on us to provide quick and accurate estimates for their project ProForma/Feasibility work. Each project has a number of costs to consider in addition to the construction costs such as due diligence, entitlement, permit, offsite improvement, common area improvement, utility connection, inspection costs, etc. United Construction has dedicated professionals who excel at collaborating with our Customers and Designers from the very beginning of each project to produce feasibility estimates early in the project development cycle that have a very tight range of accuracy.

Shipping

As one of the leading freight forwarders in the Nigeria, you can always rely on HNL first class shipping services at very competitive rates to ensure the fastest connection of your shipments by Air/Sea from Nigeria to anywhere in the world. Our main goal is to provide you with excellent services while making sure that your time and money are well preserved. By virtue of our exclusive agreements and commitments with leading carriers in both Air and Sea, we enjoy special rates on all of our shipments and confirmed space in every booking which we will pass to you to guarantee a speedy and hassle-free delivery.
